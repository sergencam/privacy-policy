Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

This privacy policy describes how Secrets of Crime (hereinafter referred to as "the Application") collects, uses, and shares personal and device data, as well as the use of third-party services.

Collected Data

  • Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, and application crash reports.
  • Usage Data: In-game statistics (such as level progression and session duration) — anonymized.
  • Advertising/Tracking Identifiers: Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID) may be collected due to advertising SDKs.

Purpose of Use

This data is used to ensure the proper operation of the application, improve performance, debug issues, and deliver targeted advertisements.

Third Parties

The application integrates the following third-party services:

  • Game Analytics — for analytics.
  • Google Play Ads — for advertising display.

These third parties have their own privacy policies and data processing practices. Please review their policies for more details.

Children

The application is not intended for children under the age of 13. If personal data from a child is collected without parental consent, it will be deleted immediately.

Data Retention and Security

Data is retained only as long as necessary. Reasonable security measures are implemented; however, absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
